Quick & Easy Pancakes

Description

Easy-to-make, fluffy, delicious pancakes that take nore more than 15 minutes to prepare.

Ingredients

  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 2 tablespoons white sugar
  • 2 teaspoons baking powder
  • 1 teaspoon salt
  • 1 egg, beaten
  • 1 cup milk
  • 2 tablespoons vegetable oil

Steps

  1. In a large bowl, mix flour, sugar, baking powder and salt.
  2. Make a well in the center, and pour in milk, egg and oil. Mix until smooth.
  3. Heat a lightly oiled griddle or frying pan over medium high heat.
  4. Pour or scoop the batter onto the griddle, using approximately 1/4 cup for each pancake. Brown on both sides and serve hot.
